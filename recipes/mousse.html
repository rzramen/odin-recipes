Blender Chocolate Mousse

Description

This blender chocolate mousse, with espresso powder added to amp up the intensity of the chocolate flavor, is for all of you chocolate lovers - simple, easy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Combine heavy cream, sugar, cocoa powder, espresso powder, vanilla extract, and salt in a powerful blender.
  2. Blend for 30 seconds.
  3. Chill for at least 1 hour.
